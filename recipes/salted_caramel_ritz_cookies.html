Salted caramel ritz cookies

Ritz cookies are the base for these no bake salted caramel ritz cookies. The sweetness of dulce de leche and chocolate provide a wonderful contrast to the salty, buttery crackers.

Ingredients
  • 60 round buttery crackers (such as RitzÂ®)
  • 1 cup canned dulce de leche
  • 1 1/2 cups dark chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 cups dark chcolate melting wafers
  • flaky sea salt (optional)
Steps
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Arrange half of the cracker, bottom sides up, on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  3. Spoon a rounded teaspoon of dulce de leche on the center of each cracker.
  4. Top with remaining crackers, top sides up. Place baking sheet in the refrigerator and chill for at least 30 minutes.
  5. In the top of a double boiler melt chocolate chips and melting wafers over medium, stirring occasionally, unti smooth.
  6. Using a fork, dip cracker sandwiches into chocolate, allowing excess to drip back into the bowl.
  7. Return sandwiches to prepared baking sheet. If desire, sprinkle lightly with flaky sea salt. Chil sanfwiches 15 minutes or until chcollate is set. Store cookies in the refrigerator.
